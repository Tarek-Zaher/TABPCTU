“Science is not only compatible with spirituality; it is a profound source of spirituality.”
-Carl Sagan

A vast majority of all the written works in history have been dedicated to the task of creating beautiful fictional places that enstill a sense of wonder into our lives. The unfortunate truth is that every book has a last page, every movie has a final scene, and no matter how much we hate it, at some point we are forced to abandon these beautiful places and return, once again, to reality. This is not necessary. There is a beautiful place where you can stay forever, uninterrupted by a darker reality. There is a beautiful place where you are unique from everyone else. There is a beautiful place where even the most interesting and exciting possibilities you can concieve of are wildly outdone by the true nature of your surroundings.

There's a Beautiful Place Called the Universe.
